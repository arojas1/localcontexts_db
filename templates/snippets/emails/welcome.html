Welcome to the Local Contexts Hub!

What is the Hub?
The Hub is a web portal that allows communities to adapt the Local Contexts Traditional Knowledge Labels and Biocultural Labels to contextual needs so that they can be shared and implemented nationally and internationally. The Local Context Hub also allows researchers and institutions to generate Notices. The tools featured within the Hub support the proper recognition of Indigenous rights and interests through collaboration between communities and institutions and/or communities and researchers.
